Trust & safety

We know "Mexico" raises questions. Honest answers.

Private accredited hospitals. Modern infrastructure.

Every facility we use has full ICU capability, 24/7 nursing, and the same operating-room standards you'd expect at home.

Why Chihuahua?

A major Mexican city with modern private hospitals serving Mexicans, US expats, and international patients for decades. Short flights from Dallas, Houston, and Phoenix. Co-founder Oscar is from Chihuahua and has years of local relationships.

How are surgeons vetted?

Every surgeon is board-certified with verified Mexican medical credentials, specialty certifications, and significant operating experience. Many have additional US or European training. You see their full credentials before committing.

Where do surgeries happen?

At private, fully accredited Mexican hospitals — never in standalone clinics. Modern operating rooms, ICU capability, certified anesthesia equipment, and 24/7 nursing care.

What about safety in the city?

Chihuahua city is a normal place where 900,000 people live, work, and raise families. The neighborhoods we use for hospitals, hotels, and patient transport are safe and routinely used by international visitors. Your driver and coordinator stay with you.

Who's responsible if something goes wrong?

Honest answer: as a medical facilitator, SSA is not the medical provider — your surgeon and hospital are. We coordinate, advocate, and stay with you. Surgeons carry medical liability insurance. We can arrange separate medical travel insurance.

Do you support post-op care?

Yes. Two video consultations with your surgeon after you return home. If a complication arises, we help coordinate with your local doctor. Returning to Mexico for revision (if needed) is also handled through us.
